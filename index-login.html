Science Scanner

Science Scanner gathers data about innovative medical projects and presents them in standardised, clear format. It allows, investors interested in investing in med-tech projects, to browse through technologies in R&D phases stored in one place.

ScienceScanner gives also opportunity for networking between inventors to and to share their ideas. It is possible to add colleagues to technologies you are working on. Also you can link technologies with organisations which were responsible for development of given project.

Science Scanner is designed and managed by MedInvest Scanner www.medinvestscanner.com – the consultancy focused on innovative non-drug health technologies and health technology assessment. Its mission is to: identify innovations worth funding.
